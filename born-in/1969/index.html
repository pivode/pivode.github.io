World Events

Defining moments of 1969

Jul

Apollo 11 lands on the Moon - Neil Armstrong becomes the first human to walk on the lunar surface, watched by 600 million viewers worldwide

Aug

Woodstock Music Festival draws 400,000 people to a farm in upstate New York for three days of music and counterculture

Sep

Ho Chi Minh, founder of North Vietnam and symbol of Vietnamese resistance, dies at age 79

Oct

The first message sent over ARPANET, the network that will evolve into the internet - the system crashes after 'LO'

Nov

Seymour Hersh breaks the story of the My Lai Massacre, revealing US soldiers killed hundreds of unarmed South Vietnamese civilians
