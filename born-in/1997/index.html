World Events

Defining moments of 1997

Scientists in Scotland announce Dolly the sheep, the world's first cloned mammal from an adult cell, raising ethical questions globally

Hong Kong transferred from British to Chinese sovereignty after 156 years as a colony, in a ceremony watched worldwide

Aug

Princess Diana killed in a car crash in Paris; her death triggers an unprecedented global outpouring of grief

Sep

Mother Teresa dies in Calcutta just days after Princess Diana, closing a remarkable chapter in 20th century history

Oct

Asian financial crisis spreads from Thailand across Southeast Asia, wiping out trillions in market value
