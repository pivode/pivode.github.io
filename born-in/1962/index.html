World Events

Defining moments of 1962

Oct

Cuban Missile Crisis brings the world to the brink of nuclear war as the US and USSR confront each other over Soviet missiles in Cuba

Nov

China defeats India in the Sino-Indian War, reshaping Asian geopolitics

Dec

US involvement in Vietnam deepens as the first US military helicopters arrive and American advisors take a more active combat role
