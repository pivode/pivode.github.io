World Events

Defining moments of 1972

Feb

President Nixon visits China, meeting Mao Zedong and beginning normalization of US-China relations after 25 years

May

Nixon and Brezhnev sign SALT I arms limitation treaty in Moscow, the first major agreement limiting nuclear weapons

Jun

Five men arrested for breaking into Democratic National Committee headquarters at the Watergate complex in Washington

Sep

Palestinian group Black September kills 11 Israeli athletes at the Munich Olympics in a hostage siege

Dec

Apollo 17 returns from the Moon - the last humans to walk on the lunar surface, ending the Apollo program
