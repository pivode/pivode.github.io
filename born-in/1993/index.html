World Events

Defining moments of 1993

Jan

Bill Clinton inaugurated as 42nd US President; the White House launches a website for the first time

Feb

World Trade Center bombed by Islamic terrorists, killing 6 and injuring 1,000 in a parking garage explosion

Apr

Branch Davidian compound near Waco, Texas stormed by FBI; fire kills 76 including cult leader David Koresh

Sep

Oslo Accords signed by Yasser Arafat and Yitzhak Rabin on the White House lawn, creating a framework for Palestinian-Israeli peace

Oct

Battle of Mogadishu: US soldiers killed in Somalia in the 'Black Hawk Down' incident, shown live on CNN
